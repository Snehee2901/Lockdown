Janta Curfew : Beginning of Battle

A curfew of the people, by the people, for the people.

On 22nd March PM Narendra Modi announced 'Janta Curfew'.It was an attempt to contain the spread of COVID-19.PM Narendra Modi requested all the citizens to abide by the 'Janata Curfew' on March 22 from 7 am till 9 pm.During Janta Curfew people were requested to avoid going to the public places and stay in their homes.

Important announcements that were done were:
1. He requested people of the country to stay off the roads.
2. At 5 pm ,all the citizens were requested to encourage the corona warriors,by standing in their doorways and clapping and ringing bells for them.
