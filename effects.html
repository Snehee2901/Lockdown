Lockdown!!


Due to outbreak of pandermic COVID-19 not only India many other countries like Nigeria, Belgium, Ireland, Malaysia etc. have announced lockdown.Lockdown to some extent has helped to control the outbreak of COVID-19.

Positive Effects !! :

Enviornment

Enviornment has experienced the most happy change due to lockdown.

Due to the forced restrictions, pollution level in cities across the country drastically slowed down just within few days which magnetize discussions regarding lockdown to be the effectual alternative measures to be implemented for controlling air pollution.

Levels of SO2, NO2, CO, O3 and NH3 have decreased.Ozone layer has started healing.

Family Life

Due to lockdown people are getting much more family time than ever before. Family bonding has increased. Parents are able to give their children much efficient time due to lockdown.

Everyone has started playing games.People are getting enough time to work out, proper sleep , eating home-cooked food. With the lockdown people also have started healing.

Vacation

Due to lockdown many of the exams have been cancelled. Students are enjoying their vacation. Many of the online platforms like amazon prime,netflix etc. have also gained hike due to this.

According to the instructions of government unable to schools and colleges, online classes have been arranged for the students.


Negative Effects !! :

Economy

Economy has been the biggest negative effect of lockdown.Due to lockdown the country has stopped which has affected the economy the most.India's growth in the fourth quarter of the fiscal year 2020 went down to 3.1% according to the Ministry of Statistics.

During the lockdown, an estimated 14 crore (140 million) people lost employment while salaries were cut for many others.More than 45% of households across the nation have reported an income drop as compared to the previous year.The Indian economy was expected to lose over ₹32,000 crore (US$4.5 billion) every day during the lockdown 1.0 .

Migrant Workers

The effect of lockdown is devastating on the migrant workers.Lacking jobs and money, and with public transportation shut down, hundreds of thousands of migrants were forced to walk hundreds of miles back to their home villages – with some dying during the journey.

Local authorities followed with measures such as creating strict containment zones to close off hotspots, which left millions of migrant workers stranded.As of 28 May, 91 lakh migrants had travelled back home in government-arranged transport facilities.

Other

Due to lockdown mental health of people has also been affected.

Anxiety level has increased in the employees as appraisals get delayed, job loss fears rise. Insomnia, backaches, restlessness, stress and anxiety are among the side effects people are facing from prolonged work from home .

Due to lockdown children are spending excess time in mobiles and computer screens.After UNLOCK 1.0 number of COVID-19 cases have increased.
