COVID-19 DANGER


Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.Coronaviruses are a group of related RNA viruses that cause diseases in mammals and birds. In humans, these viruses cause respiratory tract infections that can range from mild to lethal. Mild illnesses include some cases of the common cold (which is also caused by other viruses, predominantly rhinoviruses), while more lethal varieties can cause SARS, MERS, and COVID-19.

       Precautions SAFE


    Wear mask

    Wear mask to prevent virus entering into your body through mouth and nose.

    Santization

    Clean and disinfect surfaces around your home and work frequently.

    Wash Hands

    Wash your hands frequently and do not touch your mouth, nose and eyes.

    Sneeze ?

    Cover your nose and mouth with a tissue or use your elbow.

    Social Distancing

    Keep at least 6 feet between yourself and others if you must be in public.

    Stay Home

    Do not step out of home unless it is important. Stay home, Stay Safe
